
MFIT subroutine / function calling tree.

M. S. Briggs, 2009 May 4


IDL calls sharable execuable MFIT.

The entry point from IDL is provided by mfit_idl.c.

mfit_idl.c calls mfit_interface.c, which calls
                  gsl_set_error_handler_off
                  and the Fortran 95 entry point MFIT:
